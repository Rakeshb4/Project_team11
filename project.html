About Us

At TasteBuds Kitchen, our love for food runs deep. We believe food is a powerful expression of culture, comfort, and creativity.

Since our humble beginnings in 2012, we've grown into a community-driven kitchen with chefs from across the globe.

Our Vision

To be India's most beloved food destination, known for taste, ethics, and creativity.

Our Mission

To deliver flavorful, high-quality meals that bring joy to every customer and support local communities.

Our Journey

  • 2012: Started in a small home kitchen.
  • 2015: Opened first outlet in Bengaluru.
  • 2018: Introduced healthy and vegan menus.
  • 2020: Launched online delivery & app.
  • 2024: Celebrated 10 million meals served!
